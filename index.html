About Us

Paleo Diet Market is the best place to find Paleo safe snacks on the web. Eating Paleo is easy when you have time to cook fresh foods, but when you are traveling, at the office, or too busy to cook, our snacks will help you stay Paleo!

The Paleo Diet Market had teamed up with a local vegan restaurant based in San Luis Obispo, CA called Bliss Cafe. We have been working with Bliss to provide Paleo dieters some of the most delicious, paleo safe, products around. All of the products we sell are 100% natural, gluten free, and compatible with the paleo way of life.

We've aggregated all the best Diet foods into one simple online store, so you don't have to buy from all over the web to stock your pantry. With one purchase, you can get all your favorite Paleo snacks.
